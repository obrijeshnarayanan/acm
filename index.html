Introduction

The ACM Student Chapter at Amrita School of Computing, Nagercoil Campus, is a vibrant community of students passionate about computer science and technology. Our chapter provides a platform for students to engage in various activities, including workshops, coding competitions, guest lectures, and collaborative projects. We aim to foster a culture of learning and innovation among our members while promoting the values of the Association for Computing Machinery (ACM).

Why Join ACM?

Joining the ACM Student Chapter offers numerous benefits, including access to exclusive events, networking opportunities with professionals and fellow students, and the chance to participate in hands-on projects and competitions. It's an excellent way to enhance your technical skills, build your resume, and connect with like-minded individuals in the field of computing.
